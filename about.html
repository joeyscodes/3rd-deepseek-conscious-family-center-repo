Who We Are

A Village of Conscious Parenting

The Conscious Family Centre (CFC) is a nature‑connected alternative education and family playgroup space in Abuja designed for children ages 0 to 10. It offers stay‑and‑play sessions, a homeschool hub, and community support for parents and caregivers.

We believe childhood should be filled with wonder, outdoor exploration, and meaningful connections. Every program is rooted in respect, intentionality, and the joy of learning. Our facilitators are trained in conscious discipline, Montessori principles, and forest school pedagogy, creating a holistic environment where every child is seen and celebrated.

Located inside the serene BMT Garden in Wuse 2, our centre offers a peaceful oasis from the city — a place where families can slow down, connect with nature, and build lasting friendships.
