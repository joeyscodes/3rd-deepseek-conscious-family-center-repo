About Conscious Family Centre

The Conscious Family Centre (CFC) is a nature-connected alternative education and family playgroup space in Abuja designed for children ages 0 to 10. We offer stay-and-play sessions, a homeschool hub, and community support for parents and caregivers.

Opening hours: Monday to Saturday 10:00 AM – 3:00 PM
📍 Location: Inside BMT Garden, opp. Legacy Centre, Wuse 2, Abuja 904101, FCT.

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you cater to?
Children ages 0–10 years. Siblings always welcome. Our stay-and-play adapts to different developmental stages.
What are opening hours?
Monday – Saturday, 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Closed Sundays for family rest.
Do parents need to stay during sessions?
For Stay & Play sessions, caregivers stay and actively participate. Homeschool hub offers flexible drop-off for older children (5+).
How do I enroll for Nanny Intensive Training?
Contact us via phone or DM on Instagram to register. Workshops happen monthly in small groups.
Is there a membership or fee structure?
Yes — pay-as-you-play, term packages, and sibling discounts available. Reach out for current rates.
