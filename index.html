About CFC

🌿 Conscious Family Centre

The Conscious Family Centre (CFC) is a nature-connected alternative education and family playgroup space in Abuja designed for children ages 0 to 10. It offers stay-and-play sessions, a homeschool hub, and community support for parents and caregivers.

📅 Opening Hours: Monday to Saturday, 10:00AM – 3:00PM

Opposite Legacy Centre, Wuse 2, Abuja — Inside BMT Garden.

✨ Our Heart & Soul

🌟 Mission: To cultivate conscious, nature-connected families through intentional play, community support, and holistic learning that honours childhood.
🌍 Vision: A world where every child grows rooted in nature, empathy, and creativity — and every family feels supported to thrive.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do you welcome?
We cater to children ages 0 to 10 years, with tailored programs for each developmental stage.
Do I need to stay during Stay & Play?
Yes, parents/caregivers stay and engage alongside children — it's a bonding & community experience.
Is the Homeschool Hub a full curriculum?
It's a supportive hub with resources, co-learning days, and parent circles — to complement your homeschool journey.
How can I register for Nanny Training?
Call our phone number or visit us at BMT Garden to enrol. Sessions run monthly.
