Our Programs

Home > Programs

Program 1

Stay & Play Sessions

Flexible drop‑in sessions where children engage in guided play, sensory activities, and social interaction under the care of trained facilitators. Perfect for building confidence and early friendships. Parents and caregivers are welcome to stay and connect with our community.

Program 2

Home School Hub & Community

A supportive network for homeschooling families. We provide shared learning spaces, group projects, and regular meet‑ups to enrich your child’s educational journey. From science experiments to literature circles, the hub is a dynamic space where families learn together.

Program 3

Nanny Intensive Training

Our certified training equips nannies with modern childcare skills, conscious discipline methods, and developmental play techniques. Graduates leave empowered and confident, ready to support families with love and professionalism. This program includes hands‑on practice and mentorship.

Program 4

Holiday Camps & S.T.E.A.M

School holiday adventures filled with science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics — all wrapped in outdoor exploration and creative projects. Each camp is themed and designed to spark curiosity while building real‑world skills. Past camps have included robotics, nature art, and kitchen science.
